Questions

Everything you need to know.

Do you travel to my location? ▼

Yes — I'm a fully mobile makeup artist and come to wherever you need me. I serve all of Los Angeles County, including Santa Monica, Beverly Hills, Malibu, West Hollywood, and the San Fernando Valley. Travel outside LA County is available for an additional fee. For destination events, contact me and we'll figure it out.

How far in advance should I book for my wedding? ▼

For bridal bookings, I recommend reaching out 3–6 months in advance, especially for peak season (May–October). Your date is only held once a deposit is received. For non-bridal services like glam and photoshoots, 1–2 weeks is usually fine, though I do fill up on weekends.

Do you do bridal trials? Are they required? ▼

Trials are highly recommended for bridal clients — they're the best way to dial in your exact look before the wedding day, test product longevity on your skin, and make sure you're 100% in love with the result. Trials are a separate 2-hour appointment and are priced accordingly. They're optional but strongly encouraged.

What brands and products do you use? ▼

My professional kit includes products from Charlotte Tilbury, NARS, Fenty Beauty, MAC Cosmetics, Armani Beauty, Laura Mercier, and more — selected for longevity, photography performance, and wide shade inclusivity. I carry 300+ shades to match every skin tone. If you have a specific sensitivity or allergy, let me know in advance and I'll make accommodations.

How long does a makeup session take? ▼

A standard glam session is 45–60 minutes. Bridal appointments are 1.5–2.5 hours depending on complexity. Editorial and multi-look shoots vary by project scope. I always build in a buffer so you never feel rushed — your time with me is entirely focused on you.
